    Objectives

    The initial objectives of the competition were to find and associate different words with different banks. As a group, the main focus was on the positive and negative words associated with each bank. After these were generated, we created a word cloud for each bank with a heat map, highlighting which words were positive and which were negative.

    Focus

    There were main points that were focused on as we went through the coding;
      * Complaints of unfair policies/fees/charges 
      * Customer attrition 
      * Unresolved issues 
      * Appeal to higher authority
      * Negative perceptions of services rendered
